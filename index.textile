h2. Welcome the to Trekking for Cancer Website

In 2011, Louis Jacques contracted with the Himalayan Glacier Trekking LLC to be part of an Expedition that begins in Kathmandu, Nepal on April 3, 2012.  The expedition is to be a 19-day trek, through the Mount Everest Base Camp at 17,462 feet, climaxing at Kala Patthar at 18,192 feet, and returning to Kathmandu, Nepal.
